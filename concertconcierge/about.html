About Concert Concierge

Concert Concierge is an online trip advisory service that allows music festival goers to create and purchase customizable travel and accommodation packages. It eliminates the stress and time spent planning the many components of attending a music festival. The current method, without our service, forces attendees to visit numerous sites such as stubhub, kayak, or hotels.com to buy their tickets, book their transportation, and find their accommodations. Concert Concierge aggregates all of this content onto one site and allows customers to customize and purchase packages either alone or with their friends.

By working with music festivals across the United States, the company will look to expand to international festivals and eventually branch out to other types of venues. Initially, the pricing strategy will consist of a service charge to the customer, a price per click percentage and then eventually we aim to include a monthly access fee for the accommodation groups that partner with Concert Concierge.
