Meet the Team

Jem Wilner CEO

Jem Wilner is a second year student at Tufts University where he is studying Drama with a minor in Entrepreneurial Leadership and a minor in Communications and Media Studies with a focus in film. The combination of these studies and his involvement as the co-founder and CEO of four separate startup teams have given him the necessary experience to understand how to proficiently act as CEO in leading a growing entrepreneurial team. With over eight years experience working in retail, Jem understands how to best communicate to customers and potential partners, as well as acting as the face of the Concert Concierge Company. His passion for music festivals is second only to his love for music. Music brings people together, and Concert Concierge brings people together for the music.

Eric Grossman CFO

Eric Grossman is a second year student at Tufts University where he is studying Economics and Entrepreneurial Leadership. He is passionate about Finance and enjoys managing his personal portfolio. The past two summers Eric worked at both sell-side and buy-side firms where he conducted industry and company specific financial analysis. His past work experience and conservative approach give him the ability help Concert Concierge make appropriate financial decisions. As CFO, his goal will be to maximize profits, while acting in the best interests of the company. Overtime, Eric seeks to increase the value of the company and provide sufficient return to potential investors.

Zoe Sobin CTO

Zoe Sobin is a 3rd year student at Tufts University where she is studying Computer Science and Entrepreneurial Leadership. These degrees and her experience working for a technology start up have given her the knowledge and experience necessary to fulfill the role of CTO for Concert Concierge. Passionate about technology and the power of great engineering, she hopes to lead an energetic and innovative team of software engineers to develop and maintain Concert Concierge's online platform. She aspires to find not only the best way to design the website for technological success, but also for aesthetics and usability.

Jamie Rodman COO and Head of Business Development

Jamie Rodman is a second year student at Tufts University where she is studying Biology and Entrepreneurial Leadership. Her degree in biology has given her experience in approaching problems in a systematic way and testing solutions to come up with the best conclusion. Jamie has experience working in the Tufts University administration in addition to managing a food retail establishment and plans to redirect these skills into her position as Chief Operating Officer and Head of Business Development. Jamie is very passionate about music and is dedicated to making the music festival experience easier for consumers so that more people can easily experience the beauty of live music.

Esperanza López Rodríguez Head of Marketing and Sales

Esperanza López Rodríguez (Marketing & Sales Head) is on her fifth year of a six year double degree in Business and Law from the Autonomous University of Madrid. The combination of both degrees has given her a deep knowledge about the structure and functioning of startups. On the law side, she has worked as an intern at the Intellectual Property Department of Gómez-Acebo & Pombo, one of the major law firms in Spain. This has granted her a unique insight into the importance of the proprietary rights. On the business side, she has always been attracted to the Marketing and Sales aspects of businesses and has specialized in this area throughout her degree. She will work to develop a vast network that attracts both customers and online travel agencies and music festivals. She will also be in charge of supervising the sales strategy and will translate her enthusiasm for music to all her daily activities.
